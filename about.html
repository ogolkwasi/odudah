AFRICAN INSTITUTE FOR MIGRATION STUDIES

While focusing on Eastern Africa, the institute aims to provide a global platform for informed global migration policy, public education, and development discussions by conducting quality peer-reviewed research using new theoretical and methodological approaches in recognition of the constantly changing process and complex patterns of human global mobility.
